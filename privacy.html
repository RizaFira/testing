Artchain — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-11-08

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Artchain ("we", "us") collects, uses, and discloses personal information when you use the Service. The Service automates posting of videos to social platforms based on user-provided content and authorizations.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account credentials and tokens: When you connect a third-party account (for example, TikTok), we store access tokens or session data necessary to perform API actions on your behalf. Tokens are stored securely and only used for the purpose you authorized.
  • Content you upload: Video files and metadata (title, description, tags) are processed to perform uploads. We do not claim ownership of your content.
  • Logs and diagnostics: We may collect usage logs and error reports to maintain and improve the Service.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to: (a) authenticate and upload content to third-party platforms with your consent; (b) operate and maintain the Service; (c) troubleshoot issues and improve functionality.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal data. We may share information with third-party service providers that perform services on our behalf (for example, hosting or analytics). We may disclose data if required by law or to protect rights or safety.

5. Data Retention

We retain tokens and user-provided content only as long as necessary to provide the Service or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us (see Contact below).

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information from unauthorized access. However, no internet transmission or storage can be guaranteed as perfectly secure.

7. Your Choices

You can disconnect third-party accounts at any time through the Service. To delete data or request access, contact us at the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. We will post changes and update the Effective Date at the top of this page.
